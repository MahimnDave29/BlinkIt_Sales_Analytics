# BlinkIt Retail Analytics Dashboard

End-to-end retail analytics project using Python, SQL, and Power BI to analyze BlinkIt's quick commerce operations and drive strategic decisions.

## Overview

Analysis of ₹18.58M revenue across 10+ outlets, 16 product categories, and 3 geographic tiers to identify growth opportunities and operational improvements.

## Key Metrics

- **Total Revenue:** ₹18.58M
- **Average Transaction:** ₹2,180
- **Top Outlet (OUT027):** ₹3.4M
- **Bottom Outlet (OUT010):** ₹0.2M (17x gap)

## Major Insights

### Geographic Performance
- **Tier 3 cities:** 41% of revenue (₹7.6M) - strongest market
- **Tier 2 cities:** 35% of revenue
- **Tier 1 cities:** 24% of revenue

### Outlet Performance
- Supermarket Type 1 drives 70% of total revenue
- Top 3 outlets contribute 42.5% of revenue
- Significant performance gap between best and worst outlets

### Product Categories
- **Top Performers:** Fruits & Vegetables (₹2.82M), Snack Foods (₹2.73M)
- **Underperformers:** Seafood (₹0.15M), Breakfast (₹0.23M)
- Low-fat products outperform regular by 1.7x across all tiers

## Recommended Actions

### 1. Market Expansion Strategy
- **Prioritize Tier 3 cities** for new outlet openings (41% revenue contribution)
- Replicate Supermarket Type 1 format (70% revenue share)
- Target 5-7 new Tier 3 locations in next 12 months

### 2. Outlet Performance Improvement
- **Immediate intervention for OUT010 & OUT019** (₹0.2M each)
  - Conduct operational audits
  - Implement OUT027 best practices
  - Review staffing and inventory management
- Benchmark all underperforming outlets against OUT027 (₹3.4M)
- Set 6-month improvement targets: achieve 50% of TOP027 performance

### 3. Product Mix Optimization
- **Expand Fresh Produce:** Increase fruits & vegetables SKUs by 20%
- **Reduce/Exit Seafood category** (₹0.15M) - redirect shelf space
- **Emphasize Low-Fat Products:** 70% higher demand - expand assortment by 30%
- **Boost Snack Foods visibility:** High impulse purchase potential

### 4. Category Management
- Relocate Fruits & Vegetables to high-traffic zones (₹2.82M category)
- Cross-merchandise snacks near checkout (₹2.73M category)
- Review breakfast category positioning or consider exit strategy

### 5. Health-Conscious Positioning
- Launch "Healthy Living" campaign emphasizing low-fat options
- Create dedicated health & wellness sections
- Partner with fitness/health brands for co-marketing

### 6. Tier-Specific Strategies
**Tier 3:** 
- Increase outlet density (highest revenue per city)
- Focus on value packs and family sizes

**Tier 1:** 
- Premium product lines
- Express delivery options
- Health and organic focus
